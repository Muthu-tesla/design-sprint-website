What is Design Sprint?

Participants will receive a problem statement through a lot system at the venue.

Students must analyze the problem and propose a smart IoT design solution using Tinkercad.

"This event encourages creative thinking, innovation, and real-world problem solving."

Sample problem statements are available in the registration form for preparation.

1

Problem Identification

Pick a random real-world challenge through our lucky draw system at the venue.

2

IoT Solution Design

Use Tinkercad to design and simulate your smart solution in just 45 minutes.

3

Pitch & Win

Present your logic and design to our judges and win exciting cash prizes.
